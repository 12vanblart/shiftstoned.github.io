The Opal Waystone Classic You are invited to an upcoming Eternal Card Game community event featuring a $300+ prize pool
co-sponsored by Shiftstoned and Eternal Warcry
WHEN: Sunday April 7th, 2019 at 12PM EST

WHERE: Kaelari's EternalTournaments.com

WATCH: Streamed live on twitch
Follow @shiftstoned

February 22, 2019 - Shiftstoned and Eternal Warcry are teaming up with the Eternal Community League and Kaelari's eternaltournaments.com to bring you the Opal Waystone Classic. Registration is free and open to everyone. Players will compete for a $300+ prize pool in this community showcase event featuring a unique format.

A Deck-building Challenge Four or more factions
The Opal Waystone gets its Power from many different colors. In order to qualify for participation, registered decks must contain a minimum of eight cards in at least four factions (, , , , ). Multi-faction cards count toward the minimum requirements for each of their colors.

Treasure Trove Cash Prizes and More
In addition to the cash prizes described below, our top three finishers will take home an original limited-edition custom wall tapestry (3' x 2') to hang in their favorite gaming spot.

1st  P L A C E: $150 plus wall tapestry

2nd  P L A C E: $100 plus wall tapestry

3rd  P L A C E: $50 plus wall tapestry



Live on twitch With Grimfan and special guests
Tune in to all the action on game day at The Great Parliament's twitch channel. Casting will be expertly provided by Grimfan, Eternal Card Game's favorite ferret-based streamer and displayer of five-faction Power - with additional special guests to be announced in the coming weeks.

Opportunities to Get Involved Together we'll find the path

In addition, Shiftstoned has many opportunites available for coders, testers, content creators or even just people to help spread the word about this event - so if you are interested in getting involved, please don't hesitate to get in touch.
