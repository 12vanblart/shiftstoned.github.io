Price of Freedom: ECQ Rundown Taking a look at the Top Eight decks from The Price of Freedom, the third official 2019 Championship Qualifier for Eternal Card Game
Power portraits for the finalists from the most recent tournament
Follow @shiftstoned


February 19, 2019 - With the conclusion of Burning Hope we are one step closer to the 2019 Eternal World Championship, and two more players have earned an invitation to the main event. Congratulations to DarkestHour and trumpets for advancing to the next round, and to all those who qualified for the ECQ.

The Top Eight
Fire was featured in every one of the Top Eight decks from the competition, with Winchest () composing more than half of lists in both the finals and the tournament overall. Yet each of the winners built their list somewhat differently.

All of the summaries here include a standout feature describing what sets each deck apart from the others.


1st  P L A C E:

► GT100's Deck (Stonescar Scrappy Hour)

19F/26S. Shadow favored for Witching Hour (SSSSSS) and Turn 4 Market cards (ICB, Burglarize). 4x Granite Waystone an obvious choice, 2x Winchest Cargo!! in favor of Seek Power. Vara's Favor.

Standout feature: Without any Justice Influence sources, Winchest Cargo will never Transmute into Wichest Contraband, but GT100 has chosen it over Seek Power perhaps with the intention of deceiving unsuspecting opponents early on by masquerading as a Winchest deck.

3rd  P L A C E:

► Stormblessed's Deck (Xenan Midrange)

20T/19S. 4x Amber Waystone. 4x Vara's Favor. Time favored for Turn 2 Teacher which can be a game winner on Turn 2. And for Worldbearer Behemoth, the only triple Influence card in the list.

Standout feature: A unique take that Stormblessed has been quietly winning with on the ladder. Now the cats out of the bag. A total of nine sites.

The landscape for Eternal looks a lot different now that it did for Burning Hope following the most recent *balance changes. It remains to be seen what new archetypes will emerge - or what older ones may resurface - in response to the update. However it seems likely that Winchest midrange will continue to be part of the mix even as it may become less dominant.
