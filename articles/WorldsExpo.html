Pictures at an Exhibition Hanging this year's Championship Power bases on the wall using the Eternal Power Calculator
Power base visualizations for all sixteen decks from the Eternal Tournament Series 2018 World Championship
December 14, 2018 - Congratulations to all of the Eternal Card Game players who qualified for RNGEternal's Eternal Tournament Series 2018 World Championship — especially to Tobboo of the Eternal Titans for their first place finish. Tobbo follows in the footsteps of another Eternal Titan, 2017 ETS World Champion camat0.

To commemorate the conclusion of the 2018 series, we have assembled here a gallery of Power base visualzations for each of the Championship decks, from Skycrag to Chalice.

As you browse the collection, check out the Eternal Power Calculator's newest feature: you can now name and title your deck, and the text will be preserved when you create a unique link to share your list with the world. (Or keep it to yourself).



May you find inspiration for your own brews as you explore the Power behind these top decks. And be sure to add a title to your next masterpiece.
