Danger: High Voltage More than half the cards in this tournament winning deck piloted by Mouche
are Power sources. I heard Alessi is still free to grow raises the question: When is
too much power just enough?






 The first step: InnovationEternal card game requries a minimum of 25 Power Cards in order for your deck to be valid. Power-hungry decks commonly include a few extras, sometimes up to 3.

At the Eternal Tournament Series recent S3W3 event, Mouche took first-place piloting a deck that includes no less than 32 Power Cards. Overall there is a grand total of 39 Power sources when you include Find the Way and Auralian Merchant.

That is over half the deck! What exactly is going on here? 
Power to those who seek itWith all that Power you might expect to see a lot of high-cost finishers such Sword of the Sky King or maybe even something like Novaquake Titan. But nope - the curve for Mouche's deck tops out at 4.

Maybe the deck has high Influence requirements then? That's not it either. There are no triple Infliuence requirements here, and the steepest Influence cost in the entire deck is  for Sword of Unity. 

If not greedy power costs or influence requirements then, what is all that Power for?

There are several reasons why an overload of Power works for this deck in particular:

  • Power ensures that impacful 1- and 2-cost units can be played on time;
  • Power fuels Awakened Student (as well as Wandering Wisp's Ultimate);
  • Power in the form of the Standards transmutes into efficient, low-cost Fast Spells which in turn can be used to fuel Ahlessi, Combrei Archmage

Power Cards must be doing something more than being used to play cards in this deck. And indeed they are. They play an important role in enabling early plays of critical role players such as Awakened Student and (Most Likely to get nerfed candidate) Teacher of Humilty. But the power sources in this deck play other roles: fueling Awakened Student pumps, standards provide more spellls to activate Ahlessi, Find the Way also powers Ahlessi, Seek Power not needed because it is a two faction deck.
