Fuel for awakened student The first step: InnovationEternal card game requries a minimum of 25 Power Cards in order for your deck to be valid. Power-hungry decks commonly include a few extras, sometimes up to 3.

At the Eternal Tournament Series recent S3W3 event, the Eternal Titan's Mouche took first-place piloting a deck that includes no less than 32 Power Cards. Overall there is a grand total of 39 Power sources when you include Find the Way and Auralian Merchant.

That is over half the deck! What exactly is going on here? 
HeaderWith all that Power you might expect to see a lot of high-cost finsihers such Sword of the Sky King or maybe even Novaquake Titan. But nope - the curve for Mouche's deck tops out at 4.

Maybe high influence requirements then? No again. There are no triple infliuence requirements here, only double. And the steepest influence cost in the entire  deck is Sword of Unity, which any Combrei deck should be able to hit at .

If not greedy power costs or influence requirements then, Power Cards must be doing something more than being used to play cards in this deck. And indeed they are. They play an important role in enabling early plays of critical role players such as Awakened Student and (Most Likely to get nerfed candidate) Teacher of Humilty. But the power sources in this deck play other roles: fueling Awakened Student pumps, standards provide more spellls to activate Ahlessi, Find the Way also powers Ahlessi, Seek Power not needed because it is a two faction deck.
